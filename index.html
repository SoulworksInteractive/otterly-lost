OTTERLY LOST IS A COZY STORY-RICH ADVENTURE

EXPLORE A VIBRANT ISLAND!

Roam lush forests, rocky canyons and sun-kissed beaches at your own pace, uncovering hidden corners and breathtaking vistas along the way.

DIVE BENEATH THE WAVES!

Swim through shimmering kelp forests, mysterious caves and forgotten shipwrecks to discover the secrets that lie beneath the waves.

GATHER TREASURES & GEAR!

Collect shells, treasures and handy gear that upgrade Otto’s abilities, letting you jump higher, glide farther and smash through obstacles.

MEET FRIENDLY ANIMALS!

Befriend a lovable cast of island animals, hear their stories and lend a paw in heartfelt quests to restore the balance of nature.

SOLVE LIGHT PUZZLES & PLATFORMING!

Enjoy gentle platforming challenges and clever puzzles that add just the right touch of playful problem-solving to your adventure.

EXPRESS YOUR STYLE!

Mix and match adorable outfits and accessories to show off your sea-otter style while you splash, glide and explore.
