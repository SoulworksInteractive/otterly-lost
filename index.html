A COZY STORY-RICH ADVENTURE

Otterly Lost is about wonder, friendship, and finding where you belong. You play as Otto, a curious sea otter who got swept away from his mountain home after a strange and sudden natural disaster.

Carried downriver and into the open sea, Otto must now find his way back through an island full of wonders, secrets, and surprises.

RESTORE THE HARMONY

Much like sea otters in nature, who play a crucial role in keeping marine ecosystems in harmony, Otto’s journey turns out to be about more than just getting home.

EXPLORE AT YOUR OWN PACE

It’s an open-world game, so you can go at your own pace and discover every corner of the island and the surrounding underwater.

Swim through mysterious caves, shipwrecks and kelp forests, glide above rocky canyons and sunny beaches, or simply roam through rich terrains and forests.

HANDY TOOLS, SOFT CHALLENGES

Otters know a thing or two about using tools. As you explore the island, you’ll gather useful gear to help you out: dig up hidden shells, smash through obstacles blocking your way, and zip across tricky gaps.

You’ll also upgrade your natural abilities, learning to swim faster, jump higher, climb easier, and glide further. Along the way, you’ll face light platforming and puzzles that add just the right touch of challenge – fun, rewarding, and never too intense.

MAKE FRIENDS ALONG THE WAY

Meet a lively bunch of island characters: funny, sweet, wise, bubbly, and even a little grumpy. Some could use your help, while others may guide you on your journey.

As you get to know them, hear their stories, and build friendships, you’ll take one step closer to restoring the island’s natural balance.

CUSTOMIZE YOUR LOOKS

Collect valuables by completing quests, solving puzzles, or simply exploring the island’s many hidden corners. Trade them in for cute outfits, quirky hats, and cozy accessories to express your otter self in style.
