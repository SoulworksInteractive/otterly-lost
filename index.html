TEETO IS AN ABSOLUTELY ADORABLE ADVENTURE 3D PLATFORMER

ABSORB & CHANGE FORMS!

Teeto can absorb objects from the environment and instantly take on their unique abilities. From powerful attacks to charming tricks, experiment with different forms to battle shadow creatures, navigate the world, and discover new ways to explore the vibrant worlds.

SINGLE PLAYER OR CO-OP!

Nearly everything is better when you get some help. Teeto is no different! Partner up to tackle the world in split screen co-op.

FIGHT THE SHADOW CORRUPTION!

The shadow creatures are malevolent beings that spread decay, draining the world of its color and life. Teeto and Navy must work together to defeat the shadow creatures, cleansing their corruption and restoring life to the world.

SAVE THE BABY MICHAELS!

Teeto loves Navy’s lost spoonfriend. One of them was named Michael. But Michael went and split himself into two, then three, then… oh gosh, who knows how many funny Bunnies really had! Now, the Michaels are lost, scattered across the world! Find them, rescue them, bring them home!

HELP OUT YOUR BUDDIES!

Along your journey, you will encounter a cast of wild and quirky characters, each with their own eccentric personalities and problems. Lend a hand, and try not get sidetracked by the hilarious and sometimes absurd side missions, quests, or unexpected detours.

COLLECT DIFFERENT OUTFITS!

Teeto can unlock a variety rich and stylish costumes, from the effortlessly cool to the downright ridiculous. Saving the world is important – but who says you can’t look fashionable while doing it?
